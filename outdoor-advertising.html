Overview

Physical Dominance in High-Traffic Zones

Out-of-Home (OOH) advertising remains the most impactful physical touchpoint to capture the attention of active, moving consumers. In a world of digital fatigue, a towering, premium billboard at a major highway node is impossible to scroll past, skip, or block.

Advertinfo owns and manages a vast network of premium, audited outdoor spaces, ranging from traditional high-scale hoarding structures to cutting-edge digital unipoles. We handle everything from structural engineering, printing, mounting, compliance filings, and night lighting checks.
